### Canteen Stall Advertisement

<b>Serangoon Secondary School</b> has a <b><u>Halal Noodle/Rice</u></b> canteen stall for occupancy. We are a single session school and have an estimated school enrolment of <u>1230</u> pupils and <u>150</u> staff.

<b>Closing Date: <mark>29 May 2026</mark>, (Friday)</b>

Interested applicants may obtain the application form from the school's Guard Room, General Office or <b>download here</b>. 

Please forward the completed form to the school by the closing date.

<hr>

<b><u>Requirements:</u></b>

<ul>
<li>Applicant must be a Singapore Citizen or Singapore Permanent Resident.</li>
<li>Applicants who have relevant experience in a school canteen or with catering experience are preferred.</li>
<li>Applicant to provide good service and quality food at reasonable prices approved by the school.</li>
<li>Applicant to be able to provide food that meet the health/nutritional standards set by the Health Promotion Board.</li>
<li>Applicant to maintain high standard of food and personal hygiene.</li>
<li>Applicant must participate in HPB’s "Healthy Meals in Schools Programme (HMSP)".</li>
<li>Before operating the stall, both applicant and stall assistant(s) must pass the WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1 conducted by SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG) accredited training providers.</li>
<li>Apply for individual Food Stall Licence from SFA.  All stallholders are required to produce the Certificate of Attainment for the WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1.</li>
</ul>

Upon successful award, the stallholder is expected to meet these requirements:

<ul>
<li>Stallholder should be able to commence operation of the stall immediately.</li>
<li>Stallholder must operate the stall from 7.00 am to 3.00 pm from Mon to Fri during term time and as and when necessary, during the school holidays.</li>
<li>New stallholder to obtain the Minimum Hygiene Grade (MHG) of 'B' within 3 months from date of commencement of the license.</li>
<li>Stallholder must be in personal attendance at the stall and cannot sublet his stall to another person.</li>
<li>Stallholder must open an account with Singapore Power Supply for the supply of water and gas.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<b><u>Submission for Application:</u></b>

Please submit completed application form with the following documents to the school's General Office:

<ul>
	<li>Proposed food menu and pricing; and</li>
<li>Photocopy of Certificate of Attainment for the WSQ Food Safety Course Level 1.</li>
</ul>

Closing date for Application: The completed application form with the required documents must be submitted to the school General Office <b><u>before 5pm on 29 May 2026, (Friday)</u></b> either by hand or post.

Late/Incomplete applications will not be considered. Only shortlisted applicants will be notified to attend an interview and food-tasting session, if applicable, at his/her own expense.
